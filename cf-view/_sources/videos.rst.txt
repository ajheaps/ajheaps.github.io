

cf-view training videos
================

A series of videos showing various features of cf-view

`Introduction <http://ajheaps.github.io/cf-view/videos/cfview_intro.mp4>`_ - Make map, vertical cross section, vector and line plots<br>
`Data transform <http://ajheaps.github.io/cf-view/videos/cfview_transform.mp4>`_ - Dimension collapses such as zonal or time mean, interpolation to another grid<br>
`View data <http://ajheaps.github.io/cf-view/videos/cfview_view_data.mp4>`_ - View and inspect data as a table of values<br>
`Properties <http://ajheaps.github.io/cf-view/videos/cfview_properties.mp4>`_- View, modify, delete and add properties to data and dimensions<br>
`Saving data <http://ajheaps.github.io/cf-view/videos/cfview_data_writing.mp4>`_ - modify and save data to a netCDF file <br>
